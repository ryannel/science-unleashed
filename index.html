How Does it Work?

1. Document submission

Contact us through email for a quotation regarding your desired editing services, together with your document. Accepted formats: .doc, .docx, .pdf, .ppt, .pptx, .xls, .xlsx. If you are in a hurry, don’t hesitate to request our express service option.

2. Editing and processing

After acceptance of your quotation, your document will be edited according to your requirements, while maintaining confidentiality of your work. All changes will be clearly indicated for your navigation.

3. Customer support

You will have direct communication access to the editor throughout the process, to ensure all queries are addressed efficiently.

4. Quality assurance

Your document will be edited by a native-English-speaker with a PhD. qualification and proven publication record. All scientific editing services provide a second round of manuscript editing at no further charge, in the rare case of rejection due to language issues.
