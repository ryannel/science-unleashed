How Does it Work?

1. Document submission

Email us with your query and deadline, and we'll discuss a quotation. Feel free to attach your document at this stage for a more detailed breakdown.

Accepted formats include: .doc, .docx, .pdf, .ppt, .pptx, .xls, .xlsx.

2. Editing and processing

When you are happy with your quotation, we will edit your document according to your requirements, while maintaining the confidentiality of your work.

All changes and comments will be clearly marked out for you.

3. Customer support

We'll keep in touch throughout the whole process to ensure all your queries are answered.

If you're in a hurry, don't hesitate to request our express service option.

4. Quality assurance

Your document will be edited by a native English speaker with a Ph.D. qualification and a proven publication record.

Your document will always be returned to you on schedule.
